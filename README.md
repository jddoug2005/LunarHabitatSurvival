# LunarHabitatSurvival

Lunar Habitat Survival (LUNAR_OS_TITAN)
Lunar Habitat Survival is a strategic, turn-based survival simulation set on a desolate moon base. Following a catastrophic event that left you as the sole survivor, you must manage resources, build life-support systems, and fend off lunar threats for 90 days until extraction arrives.

Core Gameplay Mechanics
1. Resource Management
To survive each "Cycle," you must balance four critical biometric levels:

Integrity (HP): Your physical health. If this reaches zero from combat or environmental hazards, the mission fails.

Oxygen (O2): Consumed every cycle. Depletion leads to asphyxiation.

Water (H2O): Used for hydration and system operations like the MedBay or Gardening.

Nutrients (Food): Consumed at the start of each day to generate Action Points (AP).

2. Occupations and Traits
At the start of the mission, you select a professional profile and genetic traits that alter your stats:

Occupations: Includes Engineer (build bonus), Scout (lower travel costs), Soldier (combat bonus), Miner (scrap bonus), Botanist (garden bonus), Hacker (lock bypassing), and Medic (MedBay bonus).

Traits: Options like "Genius" (double research), "Hoarder" (extra starting scrap), or "Efficient" (lower vitals tax).

3. Habitat Systems
You can scavenge scrap and electronics to build or upgrade vital systems in Habitat Alpha:

Solar Array: Generates power cells.

Atmospheric Condenser: Converts power into water.

Hydro-Garden: Produces food over a 3-day growth cycle.

MedBay: Consumes power and water to restore Integrity.

Defense Turrets: Provides automated fire during base interceptions.

4. Exploration and Combat
Expeditions: Travel to the Crashed Satellite, Frozen Crater, or Supply Drop Pods to find high-yield resources.

Hostile Intercepts: Encounter Scout Drones, Bio-Mutants, or Defense Mechs. Combat involves managing "Quick" and "Heavy" strikes or using Power for "Plasma Blasts".

Hacking: Engage in a decryption minigame to unlock encrypted caches and secure extra electronics.

Environmental Hazards
Survival is complicated by the lunar weather system, which rolls every cycle:

Clear: Standard conditions.

Radiation: Damages the player during transit or scavenging.

Solar Flare: Jams solar power generation.

Meteor Storm: Damages the habitat hull and can destroy scrap reserves or systems.

The A.U.R.A. AI
Your survival is assisted by the Automated Utility & Recovery Assistant (A.U.R.A.). AURA provides morning briefings, technical warnings about critical vitals, and commentary on your exploration progress.
